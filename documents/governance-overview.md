# Governance Overview

## Governance Goals

FraudLens adopts a SOX-aligned posture for traceability, ownership, reviewability, and change control.

## Required Governance Outcomes

- every critical entity has a named owner
- critical contracts identify required approvals
- audit and change-trace fields are standardized
- business definitions remain versioned and reviewable
- no untracked manual override path is assumed

## Critical Control Themes

- change approval for governed contracts
- ownership and stewardship clarity
- consistent naming across business and technical layers
- preservation of status history, event timestamps, and source references
- reconciliation between alert, case, and decision records
